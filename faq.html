Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Heavy-Duty I/M Program?

The Heavy-Duty Inspection and Maintenance (HD I/M) Program is a CARB initiative to ensure heavy-duty vehicles meet emissions standards.

Who needs to comply with CARB regulations?

All heavy-duty vehicles operating in California must comply with CARB regulations, including emissions testing and reporting.

How do I schedule a CARB compliance test?

You can schedule a test using our online appointment system or by contacting us directly.

What happens if my vehicle fails the test?

If your vehicle fails, we provide guidance on necessary repairs and retesting options to ensure compliance.
